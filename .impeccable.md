# Design Context — DanGeorge.studio

Synthesised from [[PRD]], [[design]], and [[claude]]. Source of truth for `impeccable` + sibling design skills when auditing or polishing this codebase.

## Users

Founders, creative directors, and growth leads who commission premium websites. They arrive sceptical — every other "agency" looks the same. They decide in the first 10 seconds whether to keep scrolling. Context: usually desktop, evening browsing on a late deadline, looking for proof that the freelancer can build something that would win a Webby rather than a template refactor.

## Brand Personality

Three words: **cinematic · crafted · opinionated.** The copy is short. Every detail is considered. The site IS the portfolio — so competence has to radiate from micro-interactions, typography, and spatial rhythm, not from case-study word-count. Never corporate, never cliché, never "Lorem ipsum."

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme:** deep teal dark. Core canvas `#0D544C`, recessed `#0B2422`, text warm off-white `#F5F5F0`, accent pink-white `#F5F0F4`, gold `#C8A55C` used sparingly.
- **Type pairing:** `Comico` (display — the signature move, everywhere high-impact headlines live) paired with `Permanent Marker` (body + nav + microtext, chunky hand-drawn personality).
- **Motion vocabulary:** cinematic scroll via Lenis; GSAP ScrollTrigger for revealed sections; Motion for component enter/exit. Easing = `power2.out` or quart/quint — never bounce. Staggers 0.05–0.15s.
- **Layout:** 1400px max container, asymmetric where it earns it, generous negative space, no card-grid monotony.
- **Anti-references:** gradient text, purple-blue AI palettes, glassmorphism-for-decoration, border-left accent stripes, hero-metric "127 projects" panels, identical icon-over-heading card grids, Space Grotesk / Fraunces / Inter defaults.

## Design Principles

1. **The chrome should disappear.** Nav + Footer + Container are scaffolding; they must be invisible until the user asks for them. No gratuitous decoration on structural elements.
2. **Micro-craft over macro-shouting.** Anti-aliased edges, 1px borders tinted toward brand hue, optical tracking, animation timing in the 200-400ms window — these accumulate into "considered."
3. **Every animation earns its movement.** If motion doesn't convey state change or reward attention, cut it.
4. **Mobile is a re-design, not a scale-down.** Never hide critical actions on narrow viewports. The hamburger full-screen overlay at #0B2422 with Comico 48px stacked links is the mobile identity, not a fallback.
5. **Contrast is the baseline, not the ceiling.** WCAG AA is the minimum floor — aim for AAA on body copy against `#0D544C`.
