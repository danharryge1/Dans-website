# Project Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
**Client Name:** DanGeorge.studio
**Business Type:** Independent web development and design consultancy specializing in premium, custom-coded websites featuring interactive, cinematic, and beautiful designs with sleek scroll animations.
**Project Goal:** Create a super impressive website that instantly makes visitors think, "Wow, if he can do this, I wonder what he can do for me." The site must stand out and expertly convey top-tier skills. 
- *The Core Mission:* Make the next potential client think "**I want this person to build my site**" within 10 seconds.
- *The Medium is the Message:* The site *is* the portfolio. It must be premium, scroll-animated, and deeply interactive, otherwise, the copy won't be convincing.
**Target Audience:** Business owners looking for a new or better website that actually helps them stand out—acting as an alternative to AI-generated sites (Lovable), basic templates (Wix, Shopify), and overly expensive $10k+ agencies.
**Timeline:** 1 week maximum (starting April 16th).
**Budget:** Free (Internal project).
